Founder-led online coaching

Speak English with clarity and confidence.

Think in English offers premium online English coaching for students, working professionals, and abroad aspirants who need better speaking confidence, stronger writing, and more reliable exam performance. Classes are founder-led, feedback is direct, and schedules are flexible enough for busy adult routines.

What the coaching covers

The programs are designed for learners preparing for IELTS, PTE, CELPIP, Duolingo, and LanguageCert, as well as professionals who want clearer communication for interviews, meetings, and career growth. Instead of generic batch teaching, the coaching focuses on the exact speaking, pronunciation, writing, and confidence issues that are slowing the learner down.

Live correction and practical feedback

Sessions are built around real-time speaking correction, structured writing review, and exam-focused practice. Learners are corrected when mistakes happen, not only at the end of class. That makes progress easier to retain because the correction is connected to the exact hesitation, grammar issue, pronunciation problem, or task mistake that caused it.

Personalized learning paths

Some learners need one-to-one IELTS coaching for a strict target band. Some need small-group spoken English classes to build consistency without getting lost in a crowded batch. Others only need writing correction, crash sessions, or help with a single exam module. The class format is adjusted to the learner's current level, goal, and weekly availability.

Who benefits most

This is especially useful for 12th-grade graduates preparing for higher studies, professionals who need confident workplace English, and test takers who understand the exam but still lose marks because of hesitation or weak structure. The goal is not just to know English better. The goal is to use it clearly under pressure.
