About Institute

Professional online English coaching built around your goal

Started in 2024, Think in English was created to make quality language coaching accessible online. We follow a simple approach: understand your current level, set a practical goal, and train with the right plan. This makes our online English speaking course and language exam coaching useful for both students and professionals.

Who we train

  • 12th graduates preparing for higher studies and interviews
  • Working professionals who need confident spoken English
  • Abroad aspirants aiming for IELTS, PTE, CELPIP, DUOLINGO, or LANGUAGECERT
